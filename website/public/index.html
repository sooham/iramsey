Robotouille

Welcome to your personal cooking assistant, Robotouille!

Robotouille allows hands and screen free cooking while following recipe instructions. This is done by leveraging recent advances in voice recognition and natural language processing. In other words Robotouille can just read out your recipe to you.

Using Robotouille allows the chef to immerse themselves into their process and cook worry free. Additional benefits include:

  1. Follows voice commands - just talk to it like another human.
  2. Works with any recipe link.
  3. Intelligently guides you through recipe step-by-step.
  4. Tracks and alerts user to timed cooking steps.
Created by Intel interns for Hacktech, Pasadena, Los Angeles.
